FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Samartha and our services.

How does Samartha automation work? +
Samartha uses advanced artificial intelligence to analyze your business processes and create intelligent workflows. Simply connect your tools, set up triggers and actions, and let our AI handle the rest. No coding required.
Can I integrate Samartha with my existing tools? +
Yes! We support integration with over 500 popular business tools including Slack, Gmail, Salesforce, HubSpot, Shopify, and many more. If you need a custom integration, our Enterprise plan includes custom integration development.
Is my data secure with Samartha? +
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and are compliant with GDPR, SOC 2, and ISO 27001 standards. Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, and we never share your information with third parties.
Do I need technical skills to use Samartha? +
Not at all! Our intuitive drag-and-drop interface is designed for non-technical users. We also provide pre-built templates for common workflows. However, if you have technical skills, you can create more advanced custom workflows.
What happens if I exceed my plan's task limit? +
We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can either upgrade to a higher plan or purchase additional task packs. Your workflows will continue running, but you'll need to upgrade to create new automations.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. Your workflows will continue to run until the end of your billing period. You can also export all your data before canceling.
